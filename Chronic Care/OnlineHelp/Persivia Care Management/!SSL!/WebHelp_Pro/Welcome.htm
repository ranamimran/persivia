Recognizing the importance of coordination and communication in treating chronic conditions, the Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) has established a monitoring system that includes defined care-coordination events and rewards providers who complete them. This incentive program encourages care providers to conduct regular care-coordination sessions with patients who have specified chronic conditions. If an authorized participating provider spends 20 minutes or more per month with a participating patient in one or more documented care-coordination interviews, the provider is eligible for an incentive payment of $41.92 per patient per month. Persivia’s Care Management and Physician Care Management modules are designed to support you and your facility as you seek to coordinate care for patients with chronic conditions, and to comply with the CMS care-management incentive program.

The idea of a workflow is used to clarify, organize, speed, and track care coordination activities, particularly the administration of care-coordination sessions with each patient who is eligible for them. The beginning of the workflow is usefully defined by securing a signed consent (Step 1 in the workflow) because it is required for each patient who participates in the incentive program.
