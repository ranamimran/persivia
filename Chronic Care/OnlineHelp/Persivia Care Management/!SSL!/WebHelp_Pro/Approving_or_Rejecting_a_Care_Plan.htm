Approving or Rejecting a Care Plan

Approve or reject the care plan using the buttons shown:

 

 

The buttons at the upper left of the Care Plan window allow you to approve or reject a care plan after you have reviewed it.  The buttons operate as follows.

 

  • Reject: Click this button to reject the selected care plan. If you reject a plan, a confirmation window appears to warn you that you are about to reject a plan, as shown

 

 

  • Save and Approve:  Click this button to approve a care plan and to save any additions that you made to it. A confirmation prompt, similar to that shown above, appears when you click Save and Approve. When you save a care plan, the action is automatically recorded in the Change Log, which maintains a record of all actions that relate to the specified patient. (The Change Log is described in the next section.
  • Export: The Export button creates an Excel file of the entire Care Plan and sends it to your download directory or to another destination that you specify. The Export button is located at the upper right of the Care Plan tab. You can view the exported version by looking in your Downloads folder or in any other storage location that you may have chosen.
  • Print: The Print command button creates a document file representing the care plan and saves it, either in a print-queue temporary file, or in a directory that you choose.
