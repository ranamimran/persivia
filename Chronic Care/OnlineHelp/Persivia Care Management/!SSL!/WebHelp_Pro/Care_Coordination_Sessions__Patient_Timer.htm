Care Coordination Sessions: Patient Timer

Care Managers may need to track and document the time spent on a care-coordination interview with a specified patient. CCM supports you in this activity by providing a Care Management Patient Timer that is embedded in the Manage Patient Workflow.  You would use the timer while you conduct a patient-coordination meeting by telephone or by other electronic means.  The Patient Timer is active only if one single patient is selected. This reflects the usual one-on-one nature of the care-coordination session.

 

To activate the Patient Timer, take the following steps

 

  • While in a Care Manager or Care Physician user role, access the Patient Workflow.
  • Select a specific patient. It must be a patient with an enrollment status of Active; it is usually a patient who has not yet had a care-coordination session during the current month.  
  • Click the Patient Time button.

 

The Patient Timer popup appears. Its purpose is to keep a running meter on an actual care-coordination session   with a patient, in real time.

 

Note: Because each care-coordination session must be for a single, specific patient, the timer embeds the same patient ID that is selected as shown:

 

 

The Timer popup is session-specific in other ways as well. In particular, today’s date appears automatically in the Date/Time field, next to the Calendar icon.

 

To start the timer, click the arrowhead as shown; to stop it, click the square icon.

 

 

Other fields are Duration and Comments. The Comments field is very important; you must enter a comment before closing the timer window after the conversation with the patient. However, you may add comments immediately after the call as well as during it.

 

When you start the timer, the indicator in the center begins to record accumulating seconds. After the value reaches one minute, the graphic arch shows a spreading band of color as time progresses.

 

 

As the second indicator increases, the value in the Duration field increases. If this call or conversation is the only one you have made this month, the two time values will be the same. The readings in the center of the picture record the total care-coordination time that has taken place during the month, including the ongoing call.

 

To save the record of the care-coordination conference, click the green Update button.
