Adding a Goal to a Patient Profile

CM makes it easy to add a goal to a patient profile by making elements available for reuse. To add a goal to a care plan, take these steps:

 

  • Ensure that the correct patient profile is displayed in the Patient Workflow Window.
  • Click to open the Goals Tab. It displays any existing goals and two buttons that allow you to add new goals, as shown below:

 

 

  • Click Add Custom to display a blank popup that lets you enter a new goal. However, you can also reuse existing goals elements that have been saved for reuse (by you or another authorized user). To view these elements, click Add Goals.

 

Examples of phrasing available for reuse appear in the resulting menu.
