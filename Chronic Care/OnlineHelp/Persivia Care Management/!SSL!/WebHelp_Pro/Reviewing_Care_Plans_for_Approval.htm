Reviewing Care Plans for Approval

When you click the Care Plan Tab, it lists all recent or local care plans for this patient. To select a specific care plan, click one of the entries in the Date and Time column on the left-hand side of the Care Plan Tab, as shown

 

 

Note that you can usefully display for approval only care plans with a status of Draft. If you try to approve a care plan that already has been approved, an error message appears, as shown below:

 

 

When you select a draft plan, the care plan information appears in the Care Plan window, as shown:

 

 

To review a care plan for approval, you need access to its detail. The Care Plan Window allows you to scroll down to view additional panes containing information.

These panes display details of the following care-plan elements:

 

  • Diagnoses: The names of the patient’s chronic conditions
  • Chronic Condition Assessment: A list of judgements made by in the past about the patient’s level of compliance with prior care plans or regimens
  • Goals: A record of care-plan goals, together with the target date and notes. The Goals pane, shown below, exemplifies the display of care-plan details in the Care Plan window:

 

 

Review the draft or pending care plan in the light of the patient information shown in the lower panes of the Care Plan Window.

 

Your clinical judgement may suggest that the patient and the care plan would benefit from one or more new elements, such as a new goal (target level of patient compliance), assessment, or intervention. The process is detailed in the section Adding a Goal to a Patient Profile.
