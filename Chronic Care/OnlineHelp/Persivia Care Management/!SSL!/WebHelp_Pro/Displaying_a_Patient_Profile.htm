Displaying a Patient Profile

To locate a single patient profile to review a care plan, it is often wise to begin with a diagnosis. To do so, narrow your display of patients by using the Diagnosis filters as described in the next section.

Filtering by Diagnoses

To enable managing patients by diagnosis or by specific diagnostic value, the Care Management screen provides a set of filters appears on the left below the Care Plan filter group. Its filter options are illustrated:

 

 

The Diagnoses filter includes many of the most common chronic-disease diagnoses:

 

  • Asthma
  • Afib/Aflutter
  • CKD
  • COPD
  • Depression
  • DM
  • Not Specified

 

The Filter pane also allows you to filter by specific diagnostic threshold. When you have chosen the filters that promise to narrow your search click Apply.

 

When you select an individual patient from the Workflow, the patient information appears in the Workflow Window, which opens with its Patient tab displayed. The tabs in the Workflow Window give you access to diverse patient information at several layers of detail. These are available to equip you to review a care plan. However, in many cases you can review a care plan by going directly to the Care Plan tab.
