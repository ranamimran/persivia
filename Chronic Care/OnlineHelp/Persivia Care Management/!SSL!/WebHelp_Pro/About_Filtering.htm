Using Filters to Select Patients

The filtering pane allows you to specify the types of patient profiles that you want to work with during your current session, based on the task at hand.

 

The process of selecting patient profiles begins with the fields in the Filter pane. A filter allows you to work with a controlled set of patient records. Each filter shows a category of patient records that you may need to perform a particular care-management task. The Filters pane appears as you enter the Care Management subsystem, on the left-hand side of the screen. It appears in the same format (with the same filters) for both the Care Manager and the Physician Care Manager.

 

 

The Care Type filter group is preset at Chronic to match the needs of most users.  (The Enrollment field is set at Active, but you can change it using the field’s dropdown menu.)

 

 

Your choices of patient profile types begin with the second field, Enrollment. This filter field allows you to select a set of patient records to work on based on the patient’s enrollment status (such as Active or Pending). Categories of enrollment are as follows:

 

  • Eligible: The patient is Eligible to be enrolled into CCM.
  • Pending Patient Consent: The Patient has been enrolled and must consent to the enrollment.
  • Active: The patient is enrolled and has provided consent. (Usually, this status indicates that a signed Patient Consent Form is among the documents that have been added to the Patient Profile).
  • Un-enrolled: The patient has been un-enrolled after the Patient was enrolled and received care.
  • Canceled: The patient's enrollment has been before it reached an Active status.
  • Suspended: The patient's enrollment has been suspended but the process is expected to resume.

 

Note that when "Eligible,” "Suspended,” "Un-Enrolled", or "Canceled" options are selected, the Care Status filter is disabled.
