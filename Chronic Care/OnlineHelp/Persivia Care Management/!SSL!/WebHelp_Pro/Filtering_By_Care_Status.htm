Filtering By Care Status

Besides selecting subsets of patients by their enrollment status, you can also select patients by their care status. Managing patient care includes tracking patients through a sequence of stages that are separated either by the accomplishment of a treatment goal or by an administrative event such as a care-plan approval. Care status filter options are as shown:

 

 

Filtering by care status may involve selecting patients whose status is any of the following:

 

  • Pending Care – Patients who have not received at least 20 minutes of care-coordination (as distinct from care in general) during the specified calendar month
  • Care Completed - Patients who have received at least 20 minutes of care-coordination (as distinct from care in general) during the specified calendar month
  • Open Alerts - Patients who have one or more open alerts
  • Pending Care Plan Approval – Patients whose latest care plan has not been approved by the assigned Care Physician or authorized delegate
  • Approved Care Plan - Patients whose latest care plan has been approved by the assigned Care Physician or authorized delegate

 

Once you have chosen your filter categories, you can create the list of patients. To do this, click Apply, as shown.

 

 

This signal selects the patient profiles that meet the selected terms; the Reset button clears the filters and allows the user to begin anew.
