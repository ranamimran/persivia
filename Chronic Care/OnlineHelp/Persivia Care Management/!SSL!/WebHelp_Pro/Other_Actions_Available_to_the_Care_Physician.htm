Other Actions Available to the Care Physician

The unique capabilities available to you as a Care Physician involve reviewing care plans for approval, rejection, or adjustment (by adding elements). However, you also can add new patients, add care plans, and add new care-plan elements such as patient goals, interventions, and appointments. Use the cross-references in Column 5 of the table to link to specific instructions for using these resources.
