Care Physician Activities and Resources

The Care Physician is uniquely authorized to approve, adjust (edit), and reject care plans. However, because approving a care plan requires an ordered array of clinical information, the Care Physician view of CM includes nearly all the tabs, fields, and option buttons that are available to the Care Manager. The operation of expansion arrows, drop-down menus, and addition buttons (marked with a plus sign), is standard across CCM. For detailed information about these access icons, see the section titled Graphics and Navigational Tools Common across CM.
