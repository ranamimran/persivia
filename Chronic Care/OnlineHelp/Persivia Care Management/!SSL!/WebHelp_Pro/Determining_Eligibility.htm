Determining Eligibility

“Managing patients” in this system is using available stored data to draft, clarify, advance, and document care plans and corresponding patient care. In many areas of management, the first task is ensuring that the patient is eligible to participate in the care coordination program.

 

Once patient data has been entered into the Patient Window, the patient information is stored and run against Persivia’s Patient Evaluation Service (PES). Patient records that meet all the conditions that define eligibility are flagged and saved to a special internal sub-register called the Eligibility Bucket. When the PES evaluation is complete, the patient’s ID appears in the Workflow with a status of Eligible.
