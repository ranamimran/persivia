Insurance Tab

The Insurance Tab displays the patient’s current insurance carrier, insurance type (Medicare, Medicaid, or Commercial), the effective date, and other such insurance information. The display is specific to the patient who is currently displayed in the Manage Patient workflow. As a Care Manager, you can view this information and update it.
